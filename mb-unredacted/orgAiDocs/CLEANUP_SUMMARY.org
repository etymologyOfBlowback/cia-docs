* Key Findings Summary

** What We Learned

1. **Vector Overlay Redactions (main.7)**
   - Pure black boxes drawn on top of image
   - Can be removed by extracting image layer
   - Solution: =pdf_to_png.py= extracts perfectly

2. **Physical/Scanned Redactions (main.6)**
   - Redactions applied to paper before scanning
   - Cannot be "removed" - they're part of the image
   - Solution: OCR around the redacted areas + web search for unredacted versions
   - No inpainting/enhancement helps with text recovery

3. **Redaction Identification**
   - =identify_redactions.py= successfully maps redaction locations
   - Creates CSV reports with coordinates
   - Useful for documentation and analysis
